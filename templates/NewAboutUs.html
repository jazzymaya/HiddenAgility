What is SYMCheck?

Welcome! SYMCheck is an online image diagnosis application that will help you identify what illness you may have. Our site, which has been implemented with our algorithm made with Django and tensorflow, has an accuracy of over 78% and can help you make an informed decision on if or when you may need a medical professional.
